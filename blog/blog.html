My Coding Journey

Posted: 02/20/18

When I think about the web… when it was invented… I begin to feel like a dinosaur, pilgrim, or pioneer of some sorts. Thank you, Sir Tim Berners-Lee, when you proposed this crazy “web” concept in 1989. Wait… 1989?? Yep. That’s when I start feeling like a dinosaur. I can’t wait to tell my hypothetical grandchildren that I grew up during the era of the internet, that “back in my day that there ‘world-wide web’ was invented.” It feels so common nature to search things on the web and I really can’t imagine my life without it. Is that sad? Meh. Send me a therapy email at a later time… for now let’s get to the point of this post. *My Coding Journey* Oo0o0o0o0 And to be quite frank with you, I’m pretty sure I am using the words coder/coding a little out of content, since I’m strictly referring to learning super basic web design/development, but for the sake of matching “Cs” (Christie—Coffee, Cats, and Coding), let’s roll with it.
